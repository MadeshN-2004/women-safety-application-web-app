About Us

Welcome to the Heroic Dashboard. Here, you'll find crucial information and tools to enhance women's safety. Our platform offers various features including emergency contacts, voice control for SOS alerts, real-time location tracking, and a user profile for personalized settings.

In case of an emergency, you can quickly access emergency contacts and send SOS alerts with just a few clicks. The voice control feature allows you to activate safety measures hands-free, and our location tracking ensures you can always be located if needed. Keep your profile updated with accurate information to ensure the best safety measures.
